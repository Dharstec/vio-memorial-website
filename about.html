About Us

Vio Memorial Academy

Vio Memorial Academy is a co- educational school established in 2005 at Vakho (New Karong, Senapati, Manipur in loving Memory of R. Vio by his children. It has fun Pre- School Class for starter child, and the succeeding classes up to X. It is located in a quiet place for peaceful learning and has good national highway connectivity. The school had a humble beginning with the name ‘Yesu Yurino Hana Kindergarten’. But with God widening its tent, we are now embracing the idea of making it a worthwhile endeavor.

  • At the Vio Memorial Academy, we all work together diligently trusting in the Promise that he who seeks diligently finds which is why we have also imbibed that truth as our motto.
  • The school was named after R. Vio as a source of inspiration. He was a nobody. But, the almighty God graciously assisted him when he sought after God’s kingdom with all his heart. And thus, God accomplished much wonderful things through his life.
  • Keeping in view that though education is an important stepping stone to serve God and humanity better down here, but not the absolute single necessity of Man, the Academy aspires to give love and care to all the students uniquely and work for their all- round development.

The administrative system has been designed for easy and friendly dealings between teachers, parents, and management. Thereby, the aspiration of all can be achieved and difficulties, if any, can be redressed harmoniously.
